### 🔌 Why Obsidian?

Obsidian feels less like an app and more like a workshop. Plugins let you bend it into whatever shape your thinking needs — and building those plugins is where I get to combine web development skills with something deeply personal: how people organize their thoughts.

### 🐧 Why Linux?

Control, transparency, and the satisfaction of understanding what's actually happening under the hood. Every broken config taught me something I now can't unlearn.
